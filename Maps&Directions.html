Directions to FAP Locations

FAP Central:

3851 Warren St,
Philadelphia, PA 19104
215-382-0293

Easily accessible from the #10 trolley line and within walking distance to many dining options, FAP-Central is located not far from its previous home on Lancaster Avenue and across the street from Saunders Park.

Please go to Septa.com for trolly schedule and fares.

FAP North:

Wyncote Comons
827 Glenside Avenue Suite 201
Wyncote PA 19095
215-887-2249

There is free parking in the lot of off Glenside Avenue behind the building. There is also a small drop-off lot at the Wyncote Commons lobby entrance on Rices Mill Road. If parking in the Glenside lot, enter the Wyncote Commons lobby and go left past the stairs to use the elevator or use the stairs, we are on the second floor.

FAP Satellite Programs: (2 locations)

1. Bryn Mawr:

Baldwin School Lower Gym
701 Montgomery Avenue
Bryn Mawr PA 19010

Bryn Mawr location only has Friday classes. All levels are welcome to join. However, there are no summer classes. Athletes will be instructed by several coaches including Maestro Mark L. Masters, Patricia Dominguez, Patrick Mifflin and Nick Minott.

2. Merion Station:

Merion Meeting Activities Center
615 Montgomery Avenue
Merion Station PA

Merion Station location only has Thursday classes which are open only to Youth fencers (8 - 12 years old). However, all levels of youth fencers are welcome to join. Athletes will be instructed by Coach Patrick Mifflin.
