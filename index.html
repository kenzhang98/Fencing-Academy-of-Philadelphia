  • FAP Central has 10 strips. Featuring SportCourt athletic flooring and an urban chic vibe, the class training room is situated in the front adjacent to to the waiting area and the weight room, the bouting room is found in the back with overhead reels and signature FAP colors.

  • FAP North has 14 strips, 10 electric strips with overhead systems, a weight room, study room, pro-shop, armoury, changing rooms and a member lounge. The class training area has 8 foot mirrors to facilitate learning. The floor is a custom sport floor installed by Snapcourt and is an excellent, high traction, cushioned surface for fencing and training on.

  • Satellite Programs are located in the Philadelphia and Main Line areas. This picture shows our Master training athletes at one of our satellite locations at Bryn Mawr, PA.
